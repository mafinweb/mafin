Actresses

Marta Belmonte

Marta Belmonte Ibáñez was born in Barcelona on September 27, 1982.

She developed her talent primarily in theater and in TV series such as "Servir y Proteger", "Isabel", "Isla Brava", "Los Favoritos de Midas" and more. She was also the author and director of the play "El orden de las cosas".

In late 2023, it was confirmed that she would star in "Sueños de libertad", an Antena 3 daytime series, playing the role of Marta de la Reina, a woman of her time, who gives voice to the struggles of a generation. Her relationship with Fina quickly became the series' most internationally popular romantic storyline.

Alba Brunet

Alba Brunet was born in Palma de Mallorca on October 18, 1993.

She furthered her acting training in productions such as "Acacias 38", such as the series "Paraíso" or "Mòpies", and short films like "Andy y las demás".

In late 2023, it was announced that she would play a co-starring role in "Sueños de libertad", the Antena 3 daily series, to bring to life Serafina Valero. Her portrayal of a brave and authentic lesbian woman in the 1950s has been key to the series' international success, thanks to her romantic relationship with Marta de la Reina, wich brought Mafin to life.
