MAFIN

Marta and Fina in "Sueños de Libertad"

Welcome to Mafin's first website! 🧁 is the official name of the couple formed by the characters Marta de la Reina Vásquez (Marta Belmonte) and Serafina Valero Izquierdo (Alba Brunet) in the series "Sueños de Libertad" ("Dreams of Freedom"), which airs Monday through Friday on Spain's Antena 3 network. Available in atresplayer online.

Data

First appearance

Marta and Fina's first private conversation takes place in episode 1 of the HBO Latam version, and in episode 5 of the original Spanish version.

Origin of the ship's name

The origin of the ship name “Mafin” 🧁 it was decided through an official poll on March 22, 2024.

Official song

MaFin has its own original song (written by Juan Navazo), which was officially unveiled on Pride Day (June 28, 2025). It was originally titled “Marta y Fina,” but in 2026 it was renamed “Pasión oculta.”

Awards

Best Couple of 2025 and Best First Kiss of 2024 at Telenovela Magazine's Telenovela Awards.
